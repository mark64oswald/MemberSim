# Provider Network Management

## Overview

Provider networks are contracted healthcare providers who agree to deliver services to plan members at negotiated rates.

## Provider Types

### Individual Providers
- Physicians (MD, DO)
- Nurse practitioners (NP)
- Physician assistants (PA)
- Therapists (PT, OT, ST)
- Behavioral health (psychologists, LCSW)

### Facility Providers
- Acute care hospitals
- Ambulatory surgery centers
- Skilled nursing facilities
- Home health agencies
- Urgent care centers
- Labs and imaging centers

### Ancillary Providers
- DME suppliers
- Pharmacy
- Transportation
- Vision/dental (if carved in)

## Network Designations

### Par (Participating)
- Contracted with health plan
- Accepts negotiated rates
- Member has lower cost sharing

### Non-Par (Non-Participating)
- No contract with health plan
- May balance bill member
- Higher member cost sharing (or not covered)

### Tier Levels
Some plans have tiered networks:
- Tier 1: Lowest cost, highest quality
- Tier 2: Moderate cost
- Tier 3: Higher cost

## Contracting

### Fee Schedules
- Medicare-based (% of Medicare)
- Medicaid fee schedule
- Custom negotiated rates
- Case rates / per diem
- Capitation

### Contract Terms
- Effective/termination dates
- Reimbursement methodology
- Quality requirements
- Credentialing standards
- Claims submission requirements

## Credentialing

### Initial Credentialing
- License verification
- DEA registration
- Malpractice history
- Board certification
- Work history
- References
- Sanctions check (OIG, SAM)

### Re-credentialing
- Every 3 years (NCQA standard)
- Ongoing monitoring between cycles

## Network Adequacy

CMS and state requirements for:
- Time/distance standards
- Provider-to-member ratios
- Appointment availability
- Specialty access

## Provider Data

### Key Attributes
- NPI (National Provider Identifier)
- Tax ID
- Specialty/taxonomy codes
- Practice locations
- Accepting new patients
- Languages spoken
- Hospital affiliations

### Directory Requirements
- Accurate and up-to-date
- Accessible to members
- Updated within specific timeframes
